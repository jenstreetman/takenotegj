TAKE NOTE COMMUNITY CHOIR

We love music

Take Note Community Choir formed in 2018 under the leadership of Director Leah Rachelle as place for people who love music, but don't necessarily have a school or church choir that they can join. Our group is a place where families can sing together. We have many members that joined with a parent, child, uncle, brother, etc., and we even have three generations of women from one family!

Take Note sings a variety of music in a range of styles from throughout the 20th Century.

Leah Rachelle

Director

Our choir director, Miss Leah Rachelle, holds a B.A. in Music and plays multiple instruments, but says she enjoys singing the most. She pursues singing/songwriting in her spare time, also teaching voice lessons and performing in the community. Check our her website leahrachelle.com
